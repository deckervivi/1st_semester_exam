Basic UX

The purpose of this theme was to learn how to create and develop a relevant digital business idea/concept – in this case a start-up agency – from the profound ideation to the launching of a digital start-up company.

The Northern Lights Hunter Application

The task was to create an innovative digital business based on a revolutionary new digital service, that we as a company have invented and made available to the users via an app.

What is it?

An application designed specifically for travelers from all over the world who want to explore the natural phenomenon of Aurora Borealis.

What are the most important features?

Forecast - It shows Hot Spots combining two important factors: solar activity and weather forecast

Plan Your Trip - Helps to find the best locations according to the user’s priorities and show accommodation and transportation options as well as other activities that the specific location offers

News feed - Join the hunters community to get the most relevant updates from other users and share your own experiences at the same time
